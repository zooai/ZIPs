# ZIP-550: Conservation Standards Alignment Matrix

## Abstract

This ZIP provides a comprehensive mapping between Zoo Labs Foundation's conservation impact framework (ZIP-500) and global standards including conservation science metrics, impact measurement frameworks, non-profit governance standards, and responsible AI principles. This matrix enables funders, partners, and auditors to verify our alignment with established frameworks.

## Purpose

As a 501(c)(3) non-profit, Zoo Labs Foundation must demonstrate:
1. **Impact legitimacy**: Alignment with conservation science standards
2. **Financial accountability**: Non-profit governance compliance
3. **Ethical operations**: Responsible AI and community protections
4. **Measurable outcomes**: Science-based metrics

This matrix serves as the authoritative reference for all compliance mappings.
