## Why Public Goods?

### Our Philosophy

1. **AI Safety Requires Openness**: Black-box AI is dangerous AI. Open research enables collective safety analysis.

2. **Science Should Be Free**: Knowledge belongs to humanity. Paywalls and patents on fundamental research harm progress.

3. **Decentralization Matters**: Centralized AI development concentrates power. Open alternatives are essential.

4. **Collaboration Accelerates Progress**: Open standards and shared infrastructure benefit everyone.

### Comparison with Other Approaches

| Organization | Model | Zoo Labs Approach |
|-------------|-------|-------------------|
| OpenAI | Closed, for-profit | Open, non-profit |
| Anthropic | Closed, for-profit | Open, non-profit |
| Meta AI | Open weights, closed training | Fully open |
| Google DeepMind | Closed | Open |
| Zoo Labs | **Fully open + public goods** | - |
