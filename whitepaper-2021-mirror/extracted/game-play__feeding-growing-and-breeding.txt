Breeding [/H]
Breeding is a pivotal feature of ZOO Labs, empowering collectors to create new and extraordinary NFT animals. By pairing two adult animals of the same species, you can generate an egg that holds the promise of a rare and exceptional creature. Breeding gives rise to a new generation, expanding your collection and opening doors to boundless possibilities.
Each generation possesses distinct breeding privileges. Initial generation animals enjoy the most significant breeding allowances, enabling them to produce offspring up to seven times. However, with each subsequent generation, the breeding privileges decrease by one. This limitation ensures a balanced and sustainable breeding ecosystem within ZOO Labs.
The breeding process involves a delicate interplay of strategy, rarity, and genetic combinations. By experimenting with diverse pairings and harnessing the genetic diversity within your collection, you have the potential to hatch extraordinary NFT animals that captivate the market and ignite enthusiasm within the community.
Feeding, cultivating, and breeding form the foundation of the ZOO Labs experience, enriching your NFT collection and immersing you in the world of digital creatures.
