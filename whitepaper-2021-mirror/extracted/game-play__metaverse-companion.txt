Zoo is a cute new game that ties in witty finance strategies into a metaphor that is a cute Tamagotchi-like game. Tamagotchi is a handheld digital pet that was first released in Japan in 1996 by Bandai. The virtual pet could be cared for by inputting commands on a keychain-sized device, which would then simulate the actions in Tamagotchi's digital world. The original release was extremely popular, with over 80 million units sold worldwide. Since its initial release, Tamagotchi has undergone many changes and evolution. Newer versions of the pet are now colorized and include new features such as the ability to connect with other Tamagotchis, play mini-games, and even go on dates. Despite the new additions, the core gameplay of caring for a virtual pet remains the same.
